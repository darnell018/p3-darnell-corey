Founded in 2015

An agency formed out of creative passion in 2015 as Darnell Creative, focused primarly on videography and photography services in the Gainesville, Florida area. This "side hustle" style business grew and transformed based on word-of-mouth over time, eventually expanding services into graphic design, web design, marketing and advertsing.

Rebranded in 2020

With business growing in size, the brand needed to be able to grow alongside. Darnell Creative was renamed to REACTIV and the rebranded to its current look in Fall 2020.
